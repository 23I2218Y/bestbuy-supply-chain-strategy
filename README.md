# Best Buy Supply Chain Strategy

A strategic case study analyzing Best Buy's competitive position against Amazon, Apple, Walmart, and Dell. Applied the VRIO framework to assess sustainable competitive advantage, evaluated omnichannel strategy performance, and developed future growth recommendations including digital health expansion and smart home partnerships. Conducted as part of the Supply Chain Strategy course (OPRE 6378) at the graduate level.

## Tools & Frameworks
- VRIO Framework
- Competitive Benchmarking
- Omnichannel Strategy Analysis
- Porter's Business-Level Strategy

## Key Insight
Best Buy's omnichannel revenue grew from 16% → 30% (2019–2022), and Geek Squad's 4M+ annual customer base represents a rare, hard-to-imitate competitive asset.
