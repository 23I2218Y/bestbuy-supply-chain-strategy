## 🧩 Problem Statement

Best Buy, a leading U.S. consumer electronics retailer, faced significant pressure from e-commerce giants like Amazon, Apple, Walmart, Target, and Dell. The challenge was to analyze Best Buy's strategic position, identify its competitive advantages, and recommend growth strategies in a mature and highly competitive industry.

---

## 🔍 What Was Done (Analysis Performed)

1. **Competitive Position Analysis**
   - Benchmarked Best Buy against Amazon, Apple, Walmart/Target, and Dell
   - Identified market share decline from 25%+ (2010) to lower levels by 2021 due to e-commerce pressure

2. **VRIO Framework Application**
   - Evaluated Best Buy's resources across Valuable, Rare, Imitable, and Organized dimensions
   - Concluded Best Buy holds a **sustained competitive advantage** via omnichannel strategy, Geek Squad, and exclusive vendor partnerships

3. **Business-Level Strategy Recommendations**
   - Addressed challenges: economic pressures, digital transformation, staffing, AI integration
   - Proposed adjustments to differentiation strategy in response to external environmental changes

4. **Future Growth Strategy**
   - Identified growth levers: digital health expansion, e-commerce strengthening, sustainability, private label, international markets, and smart home/IoT partnerships (e.g., Vivint)

## 📊 Key Findings / Results

- Best Buy's **omnichannel revenue grew from 16% (2019) to 30% (2022)**, validating its digital strategy
- Geek Squad supports **4+ million customers annually** — a rare, hard-to-imitate differentiator
- Exclusive vendor partnerships (Apple, Samsung store-within-a-store) create high barriers to imitation
- Entry into **digital health** identified as the highest-potential new revenue stream
- Recommended 6 strategic growth areas to sustain competitive advantage
